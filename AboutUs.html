Focus Areas

Public Understanding

We explain types of AI and what they can be used for if the desire outweights possible risks in plain language so everyday people can have an understanding of different types of AI and create a more informed opinion of AI and AI tools.

Independent Evaluation

We evaluate AI systems for transparency, privacy concerns, risk of dependency, and manipulation and upload these independent studies and evaluations to the public.

Responsible Standards

We study and develop possible standards for any human-AI interaction based on arrays of evidence, testing, public concern, and reported long term impact.
