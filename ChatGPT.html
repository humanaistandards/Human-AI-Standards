AI Profile

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a conversational AI developed by OpenAI. It is designed for general-purpose assistance, writing, research, coding, and education.

Developer

OpenAI

Primary Use

General AI Assistant

Availability

Free & Paid Plans

Released

2022

What is this AI?

ChatGPT is AI agent or "Chatbot" which means it is designed to take your prompts/questions and reply in an informative way based off of its own research and processes.

Strengths

  • Ease of use
  • Strong writing ability
  • Broad general knowledge
  • A good educational assistant

Limitations

  • Occasionaly provides incorrect information
  • Is confident even with misinformation
  • Replies may be based off of outdated info
  • Unable to acknowledge if a user has become overly dependent

This Ai is best for

  • Students
  • Writers
  • Simple research
  • New programmers
  • General users

Avoid if you're

  • Seeking legal advice
  • Making medical decisions
  • Emotionally vulnerable and/or in a state of high suggestibility

Human & AI Standards Summary

ChatGPT is widely used for many tasks however its not the go-to for any specific tasks. If you're looking for an assistive AI that can help you with many broad subjects (Coding, Research, Writing, Simple image creation, or just as a general Assistant) This AI is the one however as with all "ChatBot" type AI their is a risk of dependancy and because you can personalize how ChatGPT communicates the risk is high.

Always double check any information you are relying on that is obtained from an AI agent!
